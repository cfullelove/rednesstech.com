redbook.me

A simple tool to build rosters and manage the availability of your staff online.

If you run or manage a small business, redbook.me helps you by letting you know when your staff are available to work and assign them shifts.

If you are a staff member, redbook.me lets you to easily make sure your manager knows when you can work - no more being rostered on shifts you can't work!
